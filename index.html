Sample IELTS Writing Feedback

General comment:
You have shown strong effort in presenting a structured essay, and it’s clear you care about conveying your ideas clearly, even though some sentences are long and slightly awkward.

Positive aspects:
- Your essay has a clear introduction and conclusion, which frames your ideas effectively.
- You provide relevant examples, such as memorizing words and sentence structures, to support your points.

Areas to improve:
- Sentence clarity and grammar: some sentences are overly long and need punctuation adjustments.
- Word choice: avoid minor spelling errors and repetitive phrases.

Main errors with examples:
- “relocate sucessfully” → spelling mistake; should be successfully.
- “time-consuming and long-lasting process in a life of an individual” → awkward phrasing; could be a time-consuming, long-lasting process in a person’s life.
- “not matter other activities” → grammar issue; should be no matter other activities.

Band scores for criteria:
- Task Achievement: Addresses task generally, Band 6.5
- Coherence & Cohesion: Mostly clear ideas, Band 6.0
- Lexical Resource: Good vocabulary, some errors, Band 6.5
- Grammatical Range & Accuracy: Frequent small errors, Band 6.0
Overall band: 6.5

Final tips:
- Break long sentences into shorter ones for clarity.
- Double-check spelling and small grammar issues.
- Use precise phrases to replace awkward or repetitive expressions.

Tutor's comment:
This is a good effort with a logical structure. Work on your spelling and articles e.g., a, an, the. With more collocations and academic phrasing this will score higher.

Final motivating comment:
You’ve made a strong start! Keep refining your sentences and word choice, and your writing will become more polished with better clarity.
